Flooding in Eastern Bangladesh

Since 20 August 2024, heavy rainfall combined with the release of water from upstream in India has triggered severe flooding in several districts of Bangladesh, including Feni, Noakhali, Comilla, Lakshmipur, Brahmanbaria, Cox's Bazar, Khagrachhari, Chattogram, Habiganj, and Moulvibazar.The sudden floods have resulted in the deaths of 13 people, including two women.
